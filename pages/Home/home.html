Travel Tips

Pack Smart

Pack light to make your travel easier. Always bring versatile items and avoid overpacking by laying out everything you think you need, then halving it.

Stay Safe

Keep your belongings secure and be mindful of your surroundings. Use RFID wallets to protect your information and always have a backup plan in case of emergencies.

Local Cuisine

Don't shy away from trying local foods. It's a window into the culture, and you might discover some new favorites. Always ask locals for their recommendations.
